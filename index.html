The Problem

Shopify carts are
stuck in the browser

Shopify stores cart data in browser cookies. The moment a customer switches devices — it's gone. Every competitor ignores these gaps.

01

Login-only sync

Every existing app drops the ball for guest shoppers. If they haven't created an account, their cart vanishes — no matter which app you've installed.

02

Dumb merge logic

Two carts on two devices — most apps just overwrite one with the other. Items get lost. Customers get confused. Sales get missed.

03

No analytics

Merchants are flying blind. How many carts were recovered? How much revenue was saved? Which products are most commonly abandoned? No one tracks it.

04

Zero personalization

A customer returns after 3 days with a cart full of items — no app takes advantage of that moment to nudge, alert, or personalize the experience.

How It Works

Dead simple for merchants.
Invisible to shoppers.

Install once in seconds. CartSync Pro handles everything silently in the background.

1

Install & Enable

Add CartSync Pro from the Shopify App Store. One-click Theme Extension install — no code, no theme editing required.

2

We Watch Every Cart

Our JavaScript extension listens to every cart change and silently saves a snapshot to our cloud — keyed to the customer's account or email hash.

3

Cart Restored. Revenue Saved.

When the customer logs in on any device, we intelligently merge and restore their cart. They see a gentle "Your cart is back ✓" toast. You see the sale.

D2 — Smart Merge Engine

Intelligent cart merging,
not just overwriting

When a customer has carts on two devices, most apps flip a coin. We run a smart merge: union all items, take the max quantity, validate stock, and flag price changes — all before restoration.

  • Keeps the highest quantity on conflicts
  • Checks stock before restoring each item
  • Flags price drops with a celebratory nudge
  • Removes unavailable variants gracefully

D1 — Guest Mode Sync

Works even without an account

We hash the customer's email (SHA-256) to create an anonymous fingerprint. Guest cart saved on mobile? Enter the same email at checkout on desktop — it's there. No account needed. No raw email stored.

  • Privacy-first: only irreversible hashes stored
  • Triggered on checkout email entry
  • Opt-in "Save My Cart" widget available
D3 — Deep Analytics

See exactly how much you're recovering

A merchant dashboard that tells you: carts synced per day, revenue recovered, top products abandoned cross-device, and device switching patterns. Finally, proof that it's working.

  • Revenue recovered metric per period
  • Mobile → Desktop switch breakdowns
  • Top recovered products report
D4 — AI Nudge Engine
